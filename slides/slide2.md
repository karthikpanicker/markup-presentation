### HTTP Cache Headers

#### Cache-Control: private, max-age=0, no-cache; etag: <value>; expires:<value>; last-modified: <value>

* private vs public
* no-cache, no-store
* max-age
* s-maxage
* must-revalidate and proxy-revalidate
* Validators - ETag, If-None-Match

pragma, expires (legacy headers)
